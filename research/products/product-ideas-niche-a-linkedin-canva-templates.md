## Product 1 — Consultant LinkedIn Post & Carousel Pack
**Priority:** Week 1 — Starter listing
**Price:** $9–$12
**Target keyword:** consultant linkedin templates canva

### What it is
15–20 editable Canva slides covering feed posts, carousels, and thought leadership layouts designed specifically for independent consultants and freelancers.

### What's included
- 10 single-image post templates (tips, quotes, stats, announcements)
- 5–8 multi-slide carousel templates (how-to, frameworks, case study, list posts)
- 2 LinkedIn banner / cover image templates
- All slides in 1080x1080px (feed) and 1920x1080px (banner)
- Canva free tier compatible

### Why it wins
Enters at the lowest-competition keyword in the niche (129 listings). Consultant is a specific buyer with professional budget and a clear pain point — they need to look polished on LinkedIn without hiring a designer.

### Build notes
Build in a dark navy + white + gold or teal colour scheme. Corporate but not boring. Avoid generic gradients. Text-heavy layouts work well for thought leadership content.

## Product 3 — LinkedIn Banner & Profile Cover Templates
**Priority:** Week 1 — Quick build
**Price:** $5–$8
**Target keyword:** linkedin banner template canva

### What it is
10 editable LinkedIn personal profile banners for consultants, coaches, and freelancers. Multiple colour schemes included.

### What's included
- 10 banner templates at 1584x396px (LinkedIn spec)
- 5 colour scheme variants per template
- Placeholder text for name, title, and tagline
- Canva free tier compatible

### Why it wins
Fastest build in the catalog — a banner pack can be completed in a few hours. Low price point drives volume and reviews early. 715 listings competition is manageable. Buyers often purchase banners + post templates together, creating a natural bundle upsell path.

### Build notes
Focus on professional and personal branding aesthetics. Include both photo and no-photo layout variants. Minimalist, text-forward designs outperform decorative ones in this niche.
