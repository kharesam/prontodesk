## Product 1 — Etsy Seller Bookkeeping Spreadsheet
**Priority:** Week 2 — Starter listing
**Price:** $7–$12
**Target keyword:** etsy seller bookkeeping spreadsheet / etsy income tracker google sheets

### What it is
A Google Sheets bookkeeping tool built specifically for Etsy sellers. Tracks income and expenses, calculates Etsy fees automatically, prepares a Schedule C summary for US tax filing, and includes a product pricing calculator.

### What's included
- Income tracker (sales by listing, by month)
- Expense tracker (Etsy fees, materials, shipping, tools)
- Etsy fee auto-calculator (6.5% transaction + listing fee)
- Product pricing calculator (materials + time + overhead + margin)
- Monthly profit & loss summary tab
- Schedule C category breakdown for tax prep
- Instructions tab

### Why it wins
Meta buyer — an Etsy seller is already on Etsy, highly motivated, and searching with specific intent. SageDigitalTemplates validated this exact product in their 2-month rise. 343 sales in 2 months confirms the demand. Low competition at the specific keyword level.

### Build notes
Include formulas that auto-calculate Etsy's 6.5% transaction fee + $0.20 listing fee. Add a dropdown for VAT/non-VAT to support international sellers too. Keep the UI clean — colour-coded tabs (income = green, expenses = red, summary = blue).

## Engineering advantage notes

For every sheet in this niche, functional quality beats visual design. Specific features that differentiate from design-only sellers:

- Auto-calculating fee percentages (Etsy, eBay, Poshmark, etc.)
- Conditional formatting for alerts (low stock, overdue invoices, overcommitted capacity)
- Dashboard tabs that pull data from all other sheets via IMPORTRANGE or named ranges
- Dropdown validation menus for consistent data entry
- Auto-generated shopping lists or order summaries from input data
- VLOOKUP or QUERY-based cross-sheet references

These are buildable with standard Google Sheets formulas — no scripting required — but they are beyond what most Canva/design template sellers produce.
