## Research Summary

This was the first niche research session for ProntoDesk. The goal was to identify 2 validated niches to begin product development using the Spy on Rising Stores strategy, cross-validated with InsightFactory keyword signals.

A niche is confirmed when at least 2 of 4 strategy signals converge. Both niches below cleared that bar.

## Niche 1 — LinkedIn & B2B Canva Templates ✅ CONFIRMED

### Signal 1 — Rising Store: ShapeStud
- 8 months old, 641 sales, 5.0 rating
- Entire catalog focused on LinkedIn + Instagram templates for B2B, SaaS, and corporate buyers
- Multiple listings showing 15–20+ people in cart simultaneously (active live demand)
- Top products: "Modern B2B Marketing Social Media Post + Story Templates", "Enterprise Style Social Media Canva Template Bundle", "AI & SaaS Social Media Templates"
- Only 50 listings in 8 months — lean catalog, not volume-spamming
- Price point: $7–$21 USD

### Top Tags Used by Competitors

### Buyer Persona
SaaS marketers, startup founders, B2B content creators, consultants, freelancers — people who need professional-looking LinkedIn content but don't want to hire a designer.

### Opportunity / Gap
"Consultant linkedin templates canva" has only 129 listings — extremely low competition for a keyword with clear buyer intent. No dominant player exists yet in AI/tech startup-specific Canva template sets with cohesive branding.

### Confidence Score: 8/10
Two signals confirmed (rising store + keyword demand). Competition is manageable at 2K. Sub-niche entry point at 129 listings is a clear gap.

## Niche 2 — Google Sheets Trackers for Small Business Micro-Niches ✅ CONFIRMED

### Signal 1 — Rising Store: SageDigitalTemplates
- 2 months old, 343 sales, 5.0 rating
- 28 listings covering Google Sheets tools across specific niches: Etsy seller bookkeeping, Canadian tax tracker, debt payoff tracker, wedding planner
- Etsy Seller Bookkeeping template is a standout — niche-within-a-niche with high purchase intent
- Small Business Bundle (bookkeeping + CRM + inventory + pricing) is a top performer
- Price range: $2.50–$7.50 USD (volume pricing strategy)
- 343 sales in 2 months ≈ 12 sales per listing per month at current catalog size

### Signal 2 — InsightFactory Keyword Data
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Keyword searched | `google sheets small business tracker` |
| Competition | 8,000 listings |
|30-Day Sales Signal | 🔥🔥🔥 Hot |
| Weekly sell-through | 31 of 100 listings sold in past week |
| Median price | $9.99 |
| Average price | $12.58 |
| Price range | $0.99–$45.23 |

### Supporting Signals (Etsy search page observations)
- **SavvySpreads** — "Simple Sales Tracker" — 1.2K reviews, listing age 12 months (rising)
- **Candle Making Business Template** (SerenatasJourney) — 3.3K reviews, niche-specific Google Sheets — strong proof of micro-niche demand
- **Home Bakery Business Planner** (PalmAndGraceDesigns) — 1.1K reviews — another niche-specific winner

### Target Micro-Niches (prioritised)
| Micro-Niche | Rationale |
|---|---|
| Etsy seller bookkeeping + pricing tracker | Meta but highly targeted; proven by SageDigitalTemplates |
| Home baker / cottage bakery business tracker | Validated by multiple established shops |
| Candle maker business spreadsheet | SerenatasJourney proved demand at 3.3K reviews |
| Reseller (eBay/Poshmark) inventory + profit tracker | Observed in search results, dedicated shops exist |
| Freelancer CRM + invoice tracker | High-intent buyer, clear use case |

### Buyer Persona
Solopreneurs running specific trades or side businesses — Etsy sellers, home bakers, candle makers, resellers — who need a ready-made Google Sheets system without building from scratch.

### Opportunity / Gap
The broad "google sheets tracker" category is saturated, but individual micro-niches (etsy seller, home baker, candle maker) have far lower competition. Bundle strategy — combining bookkeeping + pricing + order tracking in one product — creates upsell opportunity and a higher perceived value than single-purpose sheets.

### Confidence Score: 9/10
Highest demand signal of the session (31% weekly sell-through). Multiple rising stores in adjacent micro-niches. Engineering background gives a functional quality advantage over design-only sellers. SageDigitalTemplates proved 2-month velocity at scale.

## EverBee Notes

EverBee free tier (Hobby plan) was tested during this session. Key finding: sales estimates, revenue data, trends, and growth rate are all locked behind the paid plan ($19.99/month). Free tier provides shop names and prices only, which are available from Etsy directly.

**Recommendation:** Do not rely on EverBee free tier for this workflow. Use InsightFactory for keyword signals and manual Etsy shop browsing for rising store identification. Revisit EverBee paid tier at Month 2 once initial revenue validates the investment.

---

## InsightFactory Notes

Free tier allows 2 keyword searches per day (15 total credits on signup). Keyword research tool at `new.insightfactory.app/keyword-research` is the most useful feature — shows competition count and weekly sales signal (how many of the top 100 listings sold in the past week) without a hard paywall. Daily limit of 2 searches requires planning queries in advance.
